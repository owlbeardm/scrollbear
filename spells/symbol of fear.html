This spell functions like [_symbol of death_](/spells/symbol-of-death), except that all creatures within 60 feet of the _symbol of fear_ instead become panicked for 1 round per caster level. _Note_: Magic traps such as _symbol of fear_ are hard to detect and disable. A rogue (only) can use the Perception skill to find a _symbol of fear_ and Disable Device to thwart it. The DC in each case is 25 + spell level, or 31 for _symbol of fear_.
