Even darkvision can’t see through supernatural darkness (as [_deeper darkness_](/spells/deeper-darkness)). Bright light affects creatures with light blindness or light sensitivity. For the purpose of superseding its effects with higher-level light or darkness spells, _flickering lights_ counts as a light spell when it increases the ambient light level and a darkness spell when it decreases the ambient light level.
