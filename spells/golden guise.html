You cause an object to briefly transform into gold. Affected items gain the properties of gold (_Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Equipment_ 53) until the spell expires. Weapons reduce their hardness by half and gain the fragile quality, while piercing and slashing weapons take a –2 penalty on damage rolls (minimum 1 damage). This spell increases the affected item’s armor check penalty by 2 and decreases its armor or shield bonus by 2. Gold armor has hardness 5.
