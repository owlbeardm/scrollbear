This spell acts as the _deeper slumber_ spell, but only affects one creature of 10 Hit Dice or fewer. In addition, a creature affected by this spell awakens with no knowledge of the events that led to the spell’s casting. The target loses all memory from the 5 minutes prior to falling asleep. No effect short of a [_miracle_](/spells/miracle) or [_wish_](/spells/wish) can restore memories lost by this spell.
