A creature affected by _anticipate peril_ gains a preternatural sense of danger. The first time during this spell’s duration that the target has to make an initiative check, the creature adds an insight bonus on that initiative check equal to the spell’s caster level (maximum +5). Once this bonus applies, the effects of the spell end. #### Mythic Anticipate Peril **Source** [_Mythic Adventures pg. 84_](http://paizo.com/products/btpy8ywe?Pathfinder-Roleplaying-Game-Mythic-Adventures) Add your tier to the spell’s initiative bonus and the maximum initiative bonus the spell can grant.
