This spell functions as [_summon monster I_](/spells/summon-monster-i), except you can summon 1d3 Tiny or smaller animals, such as bats, lizards, monkeys, rats, ravens, toads, or weasels. The summoned animals must all be the same type of creature. As with animals summoned with [_summon monster I_](/spells/summon-monster-i), you may apply one alignment-appropriate template to these animals.
