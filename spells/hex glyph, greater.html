This spell functions as [_hex glyph_](/spells/hex-glyph), except you can store a hex or a major hex (but not a grand hex). You trace the glyph with incense, which must first be sprinkled with powdered diamond (as noted in the material line). [_Read magic_](/spells/read-magic) allows its caster to identify a _greater hex glyph_ with a DC 16 Spellcraft check.
