You inscribe a series of runes upon the surface of a door or around the border of an entryway. They function as a [_glyph of warding_](/spells/glyph-of-warding) (blast glyph), though unlike a [_glyph of warding_](/spells/glyph-of-warding), these runes are always visible. The runes count as a [_glyph of warding_](/spells/glyph-of-warding) for the purpose of what spells can defeat it, placing multiple glyphs in the same area, and so on.
