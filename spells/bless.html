_Bless_ fills your allies with courage. Each ally gains a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls and on saving throws against fear effects. _Bless_ counters and dispels [_bane_](/spells/bane). #### Mythic Bless **Source** [_Mythic Adventures pg. 85_](http://paizo.com/products/btpy8ywe?Pathfinder-Roleplaying-Game-Mythic-Adventures) The +1 morale bonus applies on attack rolls, weapon damage rolls, and all saving throws. Once during the spell’s duration, an affected creature can roll an attack roll or saving throw twice and take the higher result. The target must decide to use this ability before the first roll is attempted.
