This spell functions as [_naga shape I_](/spells/naga-shape-i). Additionally, if the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: burrow 30 feet, climb 90 feet, fly 90 feet (good maneuverability), swim 90 feet, blindsense 30 feet, darkvision 60 feet, low-light vision, compression, constrict, detect thoughts, dreamsight, grab, hypnosis, poison, sneak attack +2d6, and spit.
