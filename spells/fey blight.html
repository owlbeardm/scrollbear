The target is cursed, gaining the bane of the caster’s fey race (see below for details on each specific fey race’s bane and duration). A creature that succeeds at its saving throw is immune to further castings of _fey blight_ from the same type of fey for 24 hours. _Fey blight_ cannot be dispelled, but effects that remove curses function against it. Attempts to identify _fey blight_ with a skill check incorrectly identify it as [_fey boon_](/spells/fey-boon) (see the ruse descriptor on page 192 of _Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Intrigue_).
