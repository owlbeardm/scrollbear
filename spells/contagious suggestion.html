This spell functions as per [_suggestion_](/spells/suggestion), except the target can pass on the enchantment to other targets. The target is compelled to communicate your suggestion to another creature, forcing the new target to attempt a saving throw as if it were the initial target. If a secondary target successfully saves, the [_suggestion_](/spells/suggestion) effect on the initial target isn’t negated. If a secondary target fails, it is placed under the same compulsion as the initial target and can further spread the [_suggestion_](/spells/suggestion). This spell can affect a total number of Hit Dice of creatures equal to your caster level. Creatures that save against this spell cannot be affected by that particular casting of _contagious suggestion_ for 24 hours.
