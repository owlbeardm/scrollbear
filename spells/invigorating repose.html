This spell enhances your spells that bring the dead back to life. If you cast [_raise dead_](/spells/raise-dead), the target creature is restored to full hit points. If you cast [_breath of life_](/spells/breath-of-life), that spell can revive a dead target as long as you cast it within a number of rounds equal to half your caster level, and the healing effect is treated as if the spell were empowered with the Empower Spell feat. Casting any of these spells discharges the _invigorating repose_ spell.
