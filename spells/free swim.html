The target can move and attack underwater normally without the usual penalties involved with underwater combat, as if under the effects of [_freedom of movement_](/spells/freedom-of-movement). However, the target doesn’t gain any protections against grapple, paralysis, and magical effects that impede movement (such as [_solid fog_](/spells/solid-fog), [_slow_](/spells/slow), and [_web_](/spells/web)).
