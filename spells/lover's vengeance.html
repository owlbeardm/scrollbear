You inspire yourself or a lover to a vengeful rage against a chosen enemy, who must be a creature that has wronged you in some way. If cast on you, the next time you are in combat with that enemy, you gain the benefits of a [_rage_](/spells/rage) spell. If cast on a lover, he or she gains the benefits of a [_rage_](/spells/rage) spell the next time the lover is in combat against your enemy. This variant of the spell must be cast within 1 hour of an intimate encounter with the target. The [_rage_](/spells/rage) effect lasts 1 round per level. If the creature that triggers the [_rage_](/spells/rage) effect is one of your lovers or ex-lovers, the benefits granted by the rage spell double. This spell counts as a [_contingency_](/spells/contingency) spell on the target for the purpose of multiple contingent effects. Worshipers of Calistria are fond of using this spell, and many keep the effect running whenever possible.
