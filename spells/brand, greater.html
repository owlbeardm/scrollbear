This spell functions like [_brand_](/spells/brand), except that it inflicts 1d6 points of damage when applied and cannot be removed, even temporarily. In addition, a _greater brand_ blazes as bright as a torch when brought within 30 feet of a visible symbol of your faith. While the symbol is blazing, the recipient is sickened. Like the effects of [_bestow curse_](/spells/bestow-curse), a _greater brand_ cannot be dispelled, but it can be removed by any means that removes a [_mark of justice_](/spells/mark-of-justice).
