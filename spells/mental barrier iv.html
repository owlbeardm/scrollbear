This spell functions as [_mental barrier III_](/spells/mental-barrier-iii), but if you are struck by a critical hit or sneak attack, there is a 25% chance that the additional damage is negated (as _light fortification_). This does not stack with similar effects that negate the additional damage from a critical hit or sneak attack. In addition, you are immune to [_mind thrust I_](/spells/mind-thrust-i) and _II_, and you take half damage from [_mind thrust III_](/spells/mind-thrust-iii), _IV_, and _V_ (reduced to one-quarter on a successful Will save). This spell can be undercast.
