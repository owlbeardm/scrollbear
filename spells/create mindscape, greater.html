This spell functions like [_create mindscape_](/spells/create-mindscape), except it can affect more creatures, the mindscape lasts longer, and you can choose the mindscape’s magic trait. You can also direct a number of believable creatures at a time equal to your caster level. _Greater create mindscape_ can be made permanent with a [_permanency_](/spells/permanency) spell by a caster of 15th level or higher at a cost of 17,500 gp. You and other creatures aware of a permanent mindscape can come and go using the [_mindscape door_](/spells/mindscape-door) spell.
