Driving sleet blocks all sight (even darkvision) within it and causes the ground in the area to be icy. A creature can walk within or through the area of sleet at half normal speed with a DC 10 Acrobatics check. Failure means it can’t move in that round, while failure by 5 or more means it falls (see the Acrobatics skill for details). The sleet extinguishes torches and small fires. ### By the way… The sleet, snow, and ice created by this spell does not move with a ship, but the deck is considered icy. This spell also allows a ship to make an additional saving throw to extinguish fires.
