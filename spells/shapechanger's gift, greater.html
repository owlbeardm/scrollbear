This spell functions as _shapechanger’s gift_ except the chosen form can be an animal (as per [_beast shape I_](/spells/beast-shape-i)), elemental (as per [_elemental body I_](/spells/elemental-body-i)), fey (as per [_fey form I_](/spells/fey-form-i)), humanoid (as per [_alter self_](/spells/alter-self)), monstrous humanoid (as per [_monstrous physique I_](/spells/monstrous-physique-i)), or vermin (as per [_vermin shape I_](/spells/vermin-shape-i)) and the chosen form can have a fly speed.
