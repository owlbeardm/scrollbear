This spell functions as [_death knell aura_](/spells/death-knell-aura), except a dying creature in the area cannot stabilize on its own and automatically takes 1 point of bleed damage on its turn each round. An incorporeal undead or living spirit traveling outside the body (such as a person using [_astral projection_](/spells/astral-projection) or [_magic jar_](/spells/magic-jar)) within the aura at the start of its turn takes 1d8 points of damage.
