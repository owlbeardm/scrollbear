An invisible but tangible field of force surrounds the subject of a _mage armor_ spell, providing a +4 armor bonus to AC. Unlike mundane armor, _mage armor_ entails no armor check penalty, arcane spell failure chance, or speed reduction. Since _mage armor_ is made of force, incorporeal creatures can't bypass it the way they do normal armor. #### Mythic Mage Armor **Source** [_Mythic Adventures pg. 100_](http://paizo.com/products/btpy8ywe?Pathfinder-Roleplaying-Game-Mythic-Adventures) The armor bonus increases to +6. There is a 50% chance that any critical hit or sneak attack made against the target is negated and treated as a normal hit, as if the target were wearing _moderate fortification armor_.
