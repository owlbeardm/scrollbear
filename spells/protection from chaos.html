This spell functions like [_protection from evil_](/spells/protection-from-evil), except that the deflection and resistance bonuses apply to attacks made by chaotic creatures. The target receives a new saving throw against control by chaotic creatures and chaotic summoned creatures cannot touch the target. #### Mythic Protection from Chaos **Source** [_Mythic Adventures pg. 104_](http://paizo.com/products/btpy8ywe?Pathfinder-Roleplaying-Game-Mythic-Adventures) As _mythic protection from evil_, except against chaotic creatures.
