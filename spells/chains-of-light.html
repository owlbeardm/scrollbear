A creature targeted by this spell is held immobile by glowing golden chains composed of pure light. The creature is paralyzed and held in place, but may attempt a new saving throw each round to end the effect. While held by the golden chains, a creature cannot use any sort of extradimensional travel, such as [astral projection](/spells/astral-projection), [blink](/spells/blink), [dimension door](/spells/dimension-door), [ethereal jaunt](/spells/ethereal-jaunt), [etherealness](/spells/etherealness), [gate](/spells/gate), [maze](/spells/maze), [plane shift](/spells/plane-shift), [shadow walk](/spells/shadow-walk), [teleport](/spells/teleport), and similar spells and spell-like abilities. The spell does not affect creatures that are already in ethereal or astral form when the spell is cast.
