Negative energy spreads out in all directions from the point of origin, dealing 1d8 points of damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +25) to nearby living enemies. Like other _inflict_ spells, _mass inflict light wounds_ cures undead in its area rather than damaging them. A cleric capable of spontaneously casting _inflict_ spells can also spontaneously cast _mass inflict_ spells.
