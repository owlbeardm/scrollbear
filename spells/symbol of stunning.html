This spell functions like [_symbol of death_](/spells/symbol-of-death), except that all creatures within 60 feet of a _symbol of stunning_ instead become stunned for 1d6 rounds. _Note_: Magic traps such as _symbol of stunning_ are hard to detect and disable. A rogue (only) can use the Perception skill to find a _symbol of stunning_ and Disable Device to thwart it. The DC in each case is 25 + spell level, or 32 for _symbol of stunning_.
