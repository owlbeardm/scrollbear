_Magic weapon_ gives a weapon a +1 enhancement bonus on attack and damage rolls. An enhancement bonus does not stack with a masterwork weapon's +1 bonus on attack rolls. You can't cast this spell on a natural weapon, such as an unarmed strike (instead, see [_magic fang_](/spells/magic-fang)). A monk's unarmed strike is considered a weapon, and thus it can be enhanced by this spell.
