Instead of grapple attempts, the tentacles attempt dirty trick combat maneuver checks (CMB = your caster level + 4 \[the tentacles’ Strength bonus\] + 1 \[the tentacles’ size bonus\]). Roll on the following table to determine the effect of a successful dirty trick combat maneuver check.
