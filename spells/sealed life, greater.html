This spell functions as [_sealed life_](/spells/sealed-life), except it additionally provides the benefits of [_death ward_](/spells/death-ward) and renders its targets immune to effects that extract or transfer its soul, such as [_possession_](/spells/possession) and [_trap the soul_](/spells/trap-the-soul). This spell prevents a soul from returning to its body if it has already departed, until the spell’s duration expires.
