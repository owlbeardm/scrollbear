You become warded against the mental prying of others, including the effects of divination spells such as [_detect thoughts_](/spells/detect-thoughts) and [_seek thoughts_](/spells/seek-thoughts)APG, as well as enchantment spells such as [_modify memory_](/spells/modify-memory) and [_memory lapse_](/spells/memory-lapse)APG. If another creature attempts to target you with a mind-affecting spell that detects or alters your thoughts or memories, the caster must succeed at a caster level check against a DC equal to 11 + your caster level or the spell fails. Only spells that detect or alter your thoughts or memories are blocked by this spell; effects such as [_clairaudience/clairvoyance_](/spells/clairaudience-clairvoyance), [_detect evil_](/spells/detect-evil), and [_locate creature_](/spells/locate-creature) continue to affect you as normal.
