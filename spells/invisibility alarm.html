This spell functions as [_alarm_](/spells/alarm), except it is triggered only by invisible creatures entering or leaving the warded area. The spell has a different mental alarm depending on whether the invisible creature is entering or leaving the area. This spell does not aid you in sensing or pinpointing the location of an invisible creature within the spell’s area. _Invisibility alarm_ can be made permanent with a [_permanency_](/spells/permanency) spell by a caster of 9th level or higher at a cost of 2,500 gp.
