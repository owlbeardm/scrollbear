This spell functions as [_symbol of death_](/spells/symbol-of-death), except that all creatures within 60 feet are affected with uncontrollable laughter (as [_hideous laughter_](/spells/hideous-laughter)) for 1 round per caster level. Unlike [_symbol of death_](/spells/symbol-of-death), _symbol of laughter_ has no hit point limit; once it is triggered, a _symbol of laughter_ remains active for 1 minute per caster level. _Symbol of laughter_ can be made permanent with a [_permanency_](/spells/permanency) spell by a caster of 10th level or higher at a cost of 5,000 gp.
