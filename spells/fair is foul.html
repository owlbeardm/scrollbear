You curse the target with a disfiguring or painful mutation. The target gains one random deformity from the Arcane Malignancies table (see page 10). The curse bestowed by this spell cannot be dispelled but can be removed with a [_break enchantment_](/spells/break-enchantment), [_limited wish_](/spells/limited-wish), [_miracle_](/spells/miracle), [_remove curse_](/spells/remove-curse), or [_wish_](/spells/wish) spell.
