This spell functions like [_shadow transmutation_](/spells/shadow-transmutation), except it can mimic [_greater polymorph_](/spells/polymorph--greater) instead of [_polymorph_](/spells/polymorph). The illusory attacks and special abilities of any shape assumed using _greater shadow transmutation_ deal three-fifths (60%) damage to nonbelievers, and nondamaging effects are 60% likely to work against nonbelievers.
