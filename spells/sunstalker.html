A touched creature becomes invisible (as per [_invisibility_](/spells/invisibility)) as long as it is in direct sunlight (or bright light shed by a [_daylight_](/spells/daylight) spell). If an action the target creature takes causes the invisible creature to become visible but does not end the _sunstalker_ spell’s duration, the creature can use this spell’s magic to become invisible again as a standard action as long as it stands in an area of sunlight (as defined above). The creature gains only concealment (20% miss chance) in normal light.
