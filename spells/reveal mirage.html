You can focus upon a single point within line of sight and discern if the terrain or geographic features of that point are illusory. This ability allows you a Will save to disbelieve a terrain-affecting illusion (glamer) effect from range as though you had interacted with it. In addition to affecting such spells as [_hallucinatory terrain_](/spells/hallucinatory-terrain) and [_mirage arcana_](/spells/mirage-arcana), this spell also allows you to disbelieve naturally occurring mirages.
