You pull forth idealized versions of yourself at various ages from your mind, splitting into three copies of yourself. _Threefold form_ functions as [_bilocation_](/spells/bilocation) (_Pathfinder RPG Occult Adventures 158_), except there are three of you instead of two, and each of the three bodies is under the effect of _threefold aspectAPG_ with a different idealized age. Other than the differences from [_threefold aspect_](/spells/threefold-aspect), each body shares all other effects with you, as normal for [_bilocation_](/spells/bilocation).
