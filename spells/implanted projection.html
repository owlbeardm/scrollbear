This spell functions as [_telempathic projection_](/spells/telempathic-projection)OA, except its mood-altering effects remain dormant in the target’s mind. Upon casting this spell, the caster designates a command word or phrase, of which the target retains no memory. The next creature to speak the command word or phrase to the target triggers the implanted [_telempathic projection_](/spells/telempathic-projection), treating the speaker as the original spellcaster.
