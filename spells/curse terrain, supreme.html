By touching the ground, you curse the land with seven unnatural hazards. This functions as [_lesser curse terrain_](/spells/curse-terrain--lesser), but with the effects of the grand perilous demesne curse (see page 144). #### Perilous Demesne, Grand **Type** regional curse; **Area** 18-mile-radius emanation; **Save** Will DC 22 to remove **Effect** As per major perilous demesne (see page 145), except up to seven hazards, each of CR 14 or less, imperil creatures in the area. The hazards are arranged so that if any are encountered together, the encounter’s effective CR from hazards must be no more than 15 (for instance, two CR 13 hazards could be encountered together). **Cure** [_Remove curse_](/spells/remove-curse) removes a grand perilous demesne only if followed by a casting of [_hallow_](/spells/hallow) at the effect’s center, and then casting [_disintegrate_](/spells/disintegrate) on each of the hazards before the next nightfall or midnight.
