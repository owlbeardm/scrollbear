A creature targeted by this spell is held immobile by glowing golden chains composed of pure light. The creature is paralyzed and held in place, but may attempt a new saving throw each round to end the effect. While held by the golden chains, a creature cannot use any sort of extradimensional travel, such as [_astral projection_](/spells/astral-projection), [_blink_](/spells/blink), [_dimension door_](/spells/dimension-door), [_ethereal jaunt_](/spells/ethereal-jaunt), [_etherealness_](/spells/etherealness), [_gate_](/spells/gate), [_maze_](/spells/maze), [_plane shift_](/spells/plane-shift), [_shadow walk_](/spells/shadow-walk), [_teleport_](/spells/teleport), and similar spells and spell-like abilities. The spell does not affect creatures that are already in ethereal or astral form when the spell is cast.
