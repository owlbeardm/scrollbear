This spell functions as [_contingency_](/spells/contingency), except as noted above. This spell also only comes into effect after your death and works only with certain spells. The companion spell triggers 1d6 rounds after your death. All decisions made involving the companion spell must be made when _deadman’s contingency_ is cast (including messages and recipients for spells like [_magic mouth_](/spells/magic-mouth) or [_sending_](/spells/sending)). If the spell targets an object or appears in a certain location, it must target or be centered on your corpse. The following spells can be companion spells for deadman’s contingency: [_animate dead_](/spells/animate-dead) (animating your corpse as an uncontrolled skeleton or zombie), _disintegrate, fireball, gentle repose, magic mouth, major image_ (with a duration of 3 rounds), _permanent image, sending, stinking cloud_, and [_teleport object_](/spells/teleport-object).
