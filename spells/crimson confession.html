You ward an object or an area with your personal rune or mark. You can choose for the writing to be visible or invisible, and it doesn’t harm the material upon which it is placed. A [_detect magic_](/spells/detect-magic) spell causes an invisible mark to glow and be visible. If any creature other than you touches the warded object or area and fails a Fortitude save, its skin turns bright red for 1 hour per level. This coloring cannot be washed away, but it can be dispelled. [_See invisibility_](/spells/see-invisibility), [_true seeing_](/spells/true-seeing), a _gem of seeing_, or a _robe of eyes_ likewise allows the user to see an invisible mark. A read magic spell reveals the words, if any. The ward cannot be dispelled, but it can be removed by the caster or by an [_erase_](/spells/erase) spell.
