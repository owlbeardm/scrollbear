This spell functions as [_instant summons_](/spells/instant-summons), except that you can target multiple objects. You must use a separate sapphire worth 1,000 gp for each one. For each item you target, you can touch a creature, granting that creature the ability to speak the special word for that item (each item has its own special word) while crushing the matched gem to call the item to hand. Only you or the touched creature can activate the gem or see the [_arcane mark_](/spells/arcane-mark) upon it.
