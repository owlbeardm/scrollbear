You trade places with the other target, teleporting as if you were both affected by [_dimension door_](/spells/dimension-door). Both you and the other target arrive in a square you choose in the other’s former space. If the targets are different sizes, they must appear in locations that cover previously occupied squares. As with [_dimension door_](/spells/dimension-door), after casting this spell, you can’t take any other actions until your next turn, and the other target is staggered until the end of its next turn due to the transposition.
