You change the target into an animal (as per [_beast shape I_](/spells/beast-shape-i)), humanoid (as per [_alter self_](/spells/alter-self)), or monstrous humanoid (as per [_monstrous physique I_](/spells/monstrous-physique-i)). The chosen form can’t have a fly speed. The subject’s statistics change as per the appropriate spell except the creature’s ability scores and natural armor do not change. The creature can change between its natural form and the chosen form at will as a standard action.
