This spell seals a solemn vow between two creatures. When this spell is cast, the targets must clasp hands and swear their oath in Kols’s name. The spell functions like [_mark of justice_](/spells/mark-of-justice), except as noted above and rather than being cursed, the oath-breaker gains a mark on the face indicating to all dwarves who see it that the target has broken a sacred oath, which gives the oath-breaker a –4 penalty to influence dwarves. The mark can be removed as described in the [_mark of justice_](/spells/mark-of-justice) spell, or the other target can forgive the oathbreaker, which causes the mark to vanish.
