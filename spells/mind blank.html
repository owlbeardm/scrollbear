The subject is protected from all devices and spells that gather information about the target through divination magic (such as [_detect evil_](/spells/detect-evil), [_locate creature_](/spells/locate-creature), _scry_, and _see invisible_). This spell also grants a +8 resistance bonus on saving throws against all mind-affecting spells and effects. _Mind blank_ even foils [_limited wish_](/spells/limited-wish), [_miracle_](/spells/miracle), and [_wish_](/spells/wish) spells when they are used in such a way as to gain information about the target. In the case of scrying that scans an area the creature is in, such as [_arcane eye_](/spells/arcane-eye), the spell works but the creature simply isn't detected. Scrying attempts that are targeted specifically at the subject do not work at all.
