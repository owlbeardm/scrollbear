This spell functions as [_illusion of treachery_](/spells/illusion-of-treachery) except that it also conceals your own actions (as _illusion of calmUC_) and renders invisible all spell effects and ranged attacks originating from you until they reach the attack’s target (this doesn’t cause the attack’s target to be unable to avoid the attack, as they still see the attack originating from the spell’s target).
