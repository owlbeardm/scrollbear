The subject is freed from spells and effects that restrict movement, including [_binding_](/spells/binding), [_entangle_](/spells/entangle), grappling, imprisonment, maze, paralysis, petrification, pinning, [_sleep_](/spells/sleep), [_slow_](/spells/slow), stunning, [_temporal stasis_](/spells/temporal-stasis), and [_web_](/spells/web). To free a creature from [_imprisonment_](/spells/imprisonment) or [_maze_](/spells/maze), you must know its name and background, and you must cast this spell at the spot where it was entombed or banished into the [_maze_](/spells/maze).
